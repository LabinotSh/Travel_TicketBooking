Boge, Rugove

Boge Bogë is a small mountainous village and also a holiday resort village in western Kosovo.
Bogë is located at Rugova Vallery, in the Albanian Alps mountain range on an altitude of about 1,300m – 1,400m.t

Mirusha Waterfalls, Peje

The Mirusha waterfalls are a series of waterfalls found in the Mirusha Park, in central Kosovo.
The waterfalls have, over time, created canyons and caves, which are famous throughout the country.

Novo Brdo Fortress, Novo Brdo

Novo Brdo Fortress is a medieval Serbian fortress in Kosovo.
Its ruins are located near the town of Novo Brdo, about 40 kilometres east of Pristina.
